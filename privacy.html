Information We Collect

We collect your location data to provide accurate Shabbat and holiday times for your area. This information is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync features.

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience. This includes app crashes, feature usage, and performance metrics.

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile information you choose to provide.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:
