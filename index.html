Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my location for accurate Shabbat times?

Go to Settings and enable location services. The app will automatically detect your location and provide accurate Shabbat and holiday times for your area.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings for TorahSense. Go to Settings > Notifications and ensure TorahSense has permission to send notifications.

How do I access premium features?

Premium features can be accessed through the subscription option in the app. Go to Settings > Subscription to view available plans.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes, basic calendar and holiday information is available offline. However, some features like location-based times require an internet connection.
